About Me

I’m a Data Analyst and Licensed Mechanical Engineer who transforms complex data into clear, actionable insights. With a solid foundation in SQL, Power BI, and research, I bridge analytics and real-world impact—whether it's diagnosing revenue drops, segmenting customers, or improving robotic systems.

I bring a unique blend of data storytelling, project leadership, and engineering problem-solving to every challenge. I’ve led analytics sprints, presented to policymakers and executives, and built dashboards that drive strategic decisions.

Whether I’m analyzing 340K+ transactions, segmenting buyer behavior, or increasing robot accuracy by 20%, I focus on delivering measurable results—and telling the story behind the data.
